About Blaker Travel & Tour

Discover our van rental story and meet the owner who is committed to making your next journey truly unforgettable.

Van Rental Services

At Blaker Travel & Tour, our van rental service is designed for flexibility, comfort, and convenience. We offer a modern fleet of vans for family trips, cargo transport, and luxury travel. Each van is maintained to the highest standard, so you can enjoy smooth, safe drives with plenty of space for luggage, gear, and passengers.

Whether you're planning a weekend escape or a longer adventure, our team helps you choose the best vehicle, route, and schedule. We also offer travel planning support, custom rental packages, and friendly service from start to finish.

Meet the Owner

Michael Riveral Lepiten

Michael is the founder of Blaker Travel & Tour, with over a decade of experience in travel planning and vehicle rental. Passionate about helping people explore safely and comfortably, Michael brings a personal touch to every booking.

From route recommendations to vehicle selection, the owner is hands-on in building trips that fit each client’s needs. Michael believes in honest service, flexible schedules, and making every journey memorable.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the trusted van rental partner for every traveler seeking safe, seamless, and memorable journeys. We aim to provide well-maintained vans, clear pricing, and responsive support to help every trip run smoothly from booking to return.

We serve families, corporate teams, cargo clients, and leisure travelers with flexible rental packages, thoughtful route planning, and local expertise. Our goal is to make travel planning feel easier, faster, and more reliable for everyone who chooses Blaker Travel & Tour.

By combining quality vehicles, friendly service, and a customer-first mindset, we want to inspire more people to explore with confidence, comfort, and peace of mind.
